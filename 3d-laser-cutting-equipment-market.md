<p><ul><li>Fiber Laser</li><li>Carbon Dioxide Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Laser Cutting Equipment market primarily consists of two types: Fiber Laser and Carbon Dioxide (CO2) Laser systems. Fiber lasers utilize optical fibers doped with rare-earth elements, offering high precision and efficiency for cutting various materials, especially metals. In contrast, CO2 lasers employ a gas mixture to generate light, making them suitable for cutting non-metal materials like wood, plastics, and textiles. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, enhancing precision and productivity in cutting processes.</p></p>
