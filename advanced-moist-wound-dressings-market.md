<p><ul><li>Hydrocolloids</li><li>Alginates Hydrofibers</li><li>Foam</li><li>Hydrogels</li><li>Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced moist wound dressings market is characterized by various types designed to facilitate healing. Hydrocolloids provide a moist environment and are effective for low to moderately exuding wounds. Alginates, derived from seaweed, absorb excess moisture and are ideal for heavily exuding wounds. Hydrofibers offer high absorption and conform to wound shapes. Foam dressings are suited for moderate to heavy exudate, providing cushioning and insulation. Hydrogels maintain moisture levels for dry or necrotic wounds, while films offer a protective barrier while allowing gas exchange.</p></p>
