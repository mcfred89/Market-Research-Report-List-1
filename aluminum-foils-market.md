<p><p>The aluminum foils market is segmented into three main types: heavy gauge, medium gauge, and light gauge foils. Heavy gauge foil is thicker, offering enhanced durability and heat resistance, making it suitable for cooking and industrial applications. Medium gauge foil provides a balance between strength and flexibility, ideal for food packaging and household uses. Light gauge foil, being the thinnest, is typically used for wrapping, baking, and disposable packaging, catering to cost-sensitive applications while maintaining efficiency.</p></p>
