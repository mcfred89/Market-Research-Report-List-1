<p><p>The antistatic wrist strap market is primarily categorized into corded and cordless wrist straps. Corded wrist straps connect directly to an electrical ground, providing continuous grounding during electronic handling, making them reliable for controlled environments. Conversely, cordless wrist straps utilize battery power or capacitive technology, offering flexibility and mobility without a physical connection to the ground. These options cater to different user needs, with corded straps favored in static-sensitive areas, while cordless straps are ideal for dynamic workspaces.</p></p>
