<p><p>The audio conferencing endpoint market can be divided into two main types: tabletop and installed systems. Tabletop audio conferencing devices are portable, typically used in smaller meeting spaces and offer flexibility for temporary setups. In contrast, installed audio conferencing endpoints are permanently integrated within a conference room, designed for larger assemblies and providing a more comprehensive audio experience. Both types serve unique needs, with tabletop options suitable for casual meetings and installed systems optimizing formal presentations and discussions.</p></p>
