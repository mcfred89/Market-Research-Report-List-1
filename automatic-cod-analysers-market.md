<p><p>Automatic COD analyzers measure Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD) in water testing, which indicates the amount of organic pollutants. The Potassium Dichromate Method involves oxidizing organic substances using dichromate, providing a reliable measure but often criticized for its toxicity. The Potassium Permanganate Method utilizes permanganate ions for oxidation, offering a less hazardous alternative. Other methods may include colorimetric approaches or advanced technologies like UV and electrochemical sensing, catering to diverse analytical needs in environmental monitoring and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
