<p><p>The automotive relay market includes Plug-in Relays and PCB Relays. Plug-in Relays are designed for easy installation in relay sockets, commonly used in vehicle electrical systems for functions like lighting and ignition. PCB Relays are mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, ideal for compact applications such as engine control units and infotainment systems. Both types ensure reliable electrical switching, with Plug-in Relays offering versatility and PCB Relays providing space efficiency, catering to diverse automotive electrical needs.</p></p>
