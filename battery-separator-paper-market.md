<p><p>The battery separator paper market is divided into two main types: dry separator paper and wet separator paper. Dry separator paper is primarily used in non-aqueous battery systems, offering high thermal stability and enhanced mechanical strength. In contrast, wet separator paper is utilized in aqueous systems, providing effective ion permeability and electrolyte retention. Both types play crucial roles in preventing short circuits and enhancing the overall performance of batteries, catering to the growing demand for energy storage solutions in various applications.</p></p>
