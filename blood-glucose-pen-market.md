<p><p>The blood glucose pen market primarily consists of two types: Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) and Blood Glucose Meters (BGM). CGM systems provide real-time glucose readings through sensors placed under the skin, allowing for continuous tracking and better management of blood sugar levels. In contrast, BGM devices require a finger prick to collect a blood sample for instant glucose readings. Both types cater to diabetes management but serve different user preferences for monitoring and lifestyle integration.</p></p>
