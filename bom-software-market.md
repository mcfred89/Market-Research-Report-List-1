<p><p>BOM software encompasses various market types tailored to different stages of a product's lifecycle. An Engineering BOM (EBOM) focuses on the design and specifications of a product, ensuring accurate documentation during development. A Manufacturing BOM (MBOM) details the components required for production, optimizing manufacturing processes. Lastly, a Service BOM (SBOM) outlines parts and assemblies needed for maintenance and support, facilitating effective service operations. Together, these BOM types enhance collaboration, efficiency, and lifecycle management across industries.</p></p>
