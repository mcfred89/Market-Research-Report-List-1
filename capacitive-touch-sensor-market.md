<p><strong>The Capacitive Touch Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Sensing</li><li>Projected Capacitive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive touch sensor market comprises two main types: surface capacitive sensing and projected capacitive sensing. Surface capacitive sensing utilizes conductive layers on the surface, detecting touch by measuring capacitance changes at the surface. This type is typically less sensitive and supports fewer touch points. In contrast, projected capacitive sensing uses a grid of electrodes beneath the surface, allowing for multi-touch capabilities and greater accuracy, making it ideal for smartphones and tablets. Both serve various applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
