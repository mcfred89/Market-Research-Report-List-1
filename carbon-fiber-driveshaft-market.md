<p><p>The carbon fiber driveshaft market is segmented into two main types: hollow shafts and solid shafts. Hollow shafts are lightweight and provide enhanced performance due to their reduced mass and increased torque capacity, making them popular in high-performance applications. Solid shafts, on the other hand, offer greater strength and rigidity, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles and demanding environments. Both types leverage the benefits of carbon fiber, such as high strength-to-weight ratios and corrosion resistance, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
