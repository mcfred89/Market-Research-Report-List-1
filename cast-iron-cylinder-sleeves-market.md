<p><p>The cast iron cylinder sleeves market includes two primary types: dry liners and wet liners. Dry liners are designed to fit snugly within the engine block, creating a seal that relies on friction, ideal for high-performance applications. Wet liners, on the other hand, are embedded directly in the coolant, providing better heat dissipation and easier replacement. Both types serve to enhance engine durability and performance, catering to diverse automotive and industrial applications depending on specific cooling and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
