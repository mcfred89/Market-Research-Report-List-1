<p><strong>The Confocal Laser Scanning Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-photon CLSM</li><li>Multiphoton CLSM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The confocal laser scanning microscopy (CLSM) market is categorized into two main types: single-photon CLSM and multiphoton CLSM. Single-photon CLSM employs a single wavelength of light to excite fluorescent samples, providing high-resolution images suitable for various applications. In contrast, multiphoton CLSM utilizes multiple photons to excite fluorescent molecules, enabling deeper tissue penetration and reduced photodamage, making it ideal for in vivo imaging. Both types are crucial for advanced biological research and material sciences, each offering unique advantages.</p></p>
