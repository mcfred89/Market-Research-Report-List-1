<p><p>The Copolyester market comprises various types, each with distinct properties and applications. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is known for its strength and clarity, making it ideal for bottles and containers. PCTG (Polycyclohexylene Dimethylene Terephthalate Glycol) offers enhanced impact resistance and transparency, suited for medical and consumer goods. PCTA (Polycyclohexylene Terephthalate) balances performance with thermal stability, while PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) is valued for its easy processing and excellent barrier properties, widely used in packaging. These copolyesters find applications in diverse sectors, including automotive, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
