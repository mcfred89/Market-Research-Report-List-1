<p><p>Distributed Fibre Optic Sensing (DFOS) utilizes fibre optic cables to monitor and measure physical parameters over long distances, such as temperature, strain, and pressure. The market is divided into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode fibre optics. Single-mode fibres offer higher sensitivity and longer range due to their narrower core, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision. In contrast, multi-mode fibres, with larger cores, support multiple light paths, providing cost-effective solutions for shorter distances and less critical measurements.</p></p>
