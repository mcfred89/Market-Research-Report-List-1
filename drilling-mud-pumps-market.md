<p><strong>The Drilling Mud Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-acting Pumps</li><li>Double-acting Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling mud pumps market features two primary types: single-acting and double-acting pumps. Single-acting pumps operate on one side of the piston, delivering fluid in a single stroke per cycle, making them simpler and less costly. In contrast, double-acting pumps utilize both sides of the piston, facilitating continuous fluid flow and higher efficiency by providing two strokes per cycle. This increased efficiency often makes double-acting pumps preferable for high-demand drilling operations, where performance is critical.</p></p>
