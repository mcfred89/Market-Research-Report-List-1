<p><p>The drones for the oil and gas market are categorized into three main types: fixed-wing, multiple rotor, and hybrid wing. Fixed-wing drones are ideal for covering large areas quickly and efficiently, making them suitable for pipeline inspections and surveying. Multiple rotor drones, also known as quadcopters, provide enhanced maneuverability and are perfect for detailed inspections and monitoring in confined spaces. Hybrid wing drones combine the benefits of both, offering versatility for long-distance travel and the ability to hover for close-up tasks.</p></p>
