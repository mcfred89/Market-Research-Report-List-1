<p><p>The Enterovirus diagnostic market consists of various testing types, including viral isolation testing, serology testing, and polymerase chain reaction (PCR) assays. Viral isolation testing involves culturing the virus from clinical samples to confirm an active infection. Serology testing detects antibodies in the blood to determine past infections or immune response. PCR assays identify viral genetic material, offering rapid and highly sensitive detection. Together, these methods facilitate accurate diagnosis and effective management of enterovirus infections in diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
