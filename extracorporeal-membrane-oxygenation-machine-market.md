<p><p>The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) machine market comprises several key components, including pumps, oxygenators, heat exchangers, and saturation monitors. Heat exchangers maintain optimal blood temperature, ensuring patient safety and comfort. Saturation monitors are essential for tracking oxygen levels in the blood, providing real-time data to clinicians. Together, these components enhance ECMO's effectiveness in critical care settings.</p></p>
