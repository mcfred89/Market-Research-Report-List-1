<p><p>The flexible rubber sheeting market includes various materials tailored for diverse applications. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) is renowned for its oil resistance, making it ideal for automotive and industrial uses. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) offers excellent weather resistance, suitable for roofing and outdoor applications. Chloroprene (Neoprene) sheeting provides good chemical resistance and flexibility, often used in gaskets and insulation. Other materials encompass silicones and natural rubber, addressing specific needs in sectors like construction, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
