<p><p>The flexographic printing press market comprises three primary types: Central Impression Type, In-line Type, and Stack Type. The Central Impression Type features a central cylinder that supports multiple printing units, ideal for high-volume and consistent quality print jobs. In-line Type integrates printing and converting processes in a single pass, enhancing efficiency for packaging applications. Stack Type arranges the printing units vertically, allowing for easy maintenance and setup, making it suitable for short runs and diverse substrates. Each type caters to specific production needs in the industry.</p></p>
