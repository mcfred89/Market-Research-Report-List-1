<p><strong>The Fluorometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Fluorometer</li><li>Spectrofluorometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorometer market is primarily categorized into two types: filter fluorometers and spectrofluorometers. Filter fluorometers utilize optical filters to isolate specific wavelengths of emitted light, making them ideal for routine analyses and applications requiring basic fluorescence measurements. In contrast, spectrofluorometers are more advanced instruments that employ a grating or monochromator to provide detailed spectral information across a range of wavelengths. This enables complex studies, such as analyzing multiple fluorophores or performing quantitative applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
