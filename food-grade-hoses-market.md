Silicone hoses are flexible, heat-resistant, and non-toxic, ideal for conveying hot food products and beverages. PVC hoses offer excellent clarity, lightweight properties, and resistance to abrasion, making them popular for a range of food processing tasks. Each type serves specific requirements in safety, hygiene, and performance in food handling.</p></p>
