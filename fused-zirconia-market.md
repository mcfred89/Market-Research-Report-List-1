<p><p>The Fused Zirconia market can be segmented into two primary types: Electrically Fused Zirconia and Chemically Synthesized Zirconia. Electrically Fused Zirconia is produced through high-temperature melting of zircon sand, resulting in a dense and hypoallergenic material used in various applications, notably in ceramics and abrasives. In contrast, Chemically Synthesized Zirconia is created through chemical processes such as precipitation or sol-gel techniques, offering controlled particle size and purity, making it suitable for advanced applications including dental ceramics and electronics.</p></p>
