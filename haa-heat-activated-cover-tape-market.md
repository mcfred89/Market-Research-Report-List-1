<p><ul><li>Non-conductive</li><li>Static Dissipative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HAA (Heat Activated) Cover Tape market is divided into two main types: non-conductive and static dissipative. Non-conductive cover tape is designed to insulate and protect electronic components without conducting electricity, making it ideal for sensitive applications. In contrast, static dissipative tape helps prevent the buildup of static electricity, safeguarding components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) damage. Both types are essential in electronics packaging, offering distinct benefits depending on the requirements of the application and the level of static protection needed.</p></p>
