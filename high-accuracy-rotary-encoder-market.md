<p><strong>The High Accuracy Rotary Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Absolute Rotary Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high accuracy rotary encoder market is primarily segmented into two types: incremental rotary encoders and absolute rotary encoders. Incremental rotary encoders provide relative position feedback by generating pulses as they rotate, making them ideal for applications requiring speed and direction monitoring. In contrast, absolute rotary encoders deliver specific position data by providing a unique digital code for every shaft position, ensuring precise location tracking even after power loss. Both types play crucial roles in automation and control systems.</p></p>
