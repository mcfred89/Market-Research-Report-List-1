<p><p>The Human Acellular Dermis market is divided into two main types: reticulated and non-reticulated. Reticulated Human Acellular Dermis is characterized by a porous, three-dimensional structure that supports tissue integration and enhances vascularization, making it suitable for complex reconstructive surgeries. Non-reticulated Human Acellular Dermis, on the other hand, has a denser composition, providing a more uniform surface ideal for simpler applications, such as wound healing. Both types are utilized in various surgical procedures to improve patient outcomes and promote healing.</p></p>
