<p><p>The human body composition analyzers market comprises various types, each serving unique measurement techniques. Bioimpedance analyzers assess body fat and muscle mass by measuring electrical resistance. Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA) uses low-level X-rays to provide precise bone density and body composition data. Skinfold calipers gauge fat thickness at specific sites for body fat estimation. Air displacement plethysmography calculates body volume for density assessment, while hydrostatic weighing measures underwater weight for fat estimation. Additional methods fall under "others."</p></p>
