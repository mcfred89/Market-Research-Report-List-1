<p><p>The In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) device market encompasses three main techniques: Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET), Donor Egg IVF, and Fresh Embryo Transfer. FET involves using previously cryopreserved embryos, offering flexibility and reducing the need for immediate transfer. Donor Egg IVF utilizes eggs from a donor, providing solutions for women with fertility issues. Fresh Embryo Transfer involves using embryos created from a fresh egg retrieval, maximizing the chances of immediate pregnancy. Each technique addresses different patient needs and preferences within the IVF process.</p></p>
