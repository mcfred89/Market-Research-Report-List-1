<p><strong>The Industrial Arc Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-EAF</li><li>DC-EAF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial arc furnaces are categorized primarily into two types: AC Electric Arc Furnaces (AC-EAF) and DC Electric Arc Furnaces (DC-EAF). AC-EAFs utilize alternating current to melt scrap metal, making them popular for steel production due to their cost-effectiveness and ability to handle various materials. In contrast, DC-EAFs employ direct current, providing better control over the melting process and improved energy efficiency, resulting in higher-quality steel with fewer impurities. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
