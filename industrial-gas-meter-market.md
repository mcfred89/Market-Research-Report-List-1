<p><p>The industrial gas meter market comprises various types, primarily focusing on Diaphragm Gas Meters and Turbine Gas Meters. Diaphragm Gas Meters utilize flexible diaphragms to measure gas volume, making them suitable for residential and small commercial applications due to their accuracy and reliability. In contrast, Turbine Gas Meters employ a rotating turbine to measure high flow rates, making them ideal for large industrial applications where precise measurements of gas consumption are essential. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring and managing gas usage efficiently.</p></p>
