<p><p>The Industrial Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) market includes two primary types: Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) and Rigid Intermediate Bulk Containers. FIBCs, often made of woven polypropylene, are lightweight and designed for bulk storage and transport of dry materials like grains and chemicals. They are collapsible for easy storage. In contrast, Rigid Intermediate Bulk Containers, typically constructed from plastic or metal, offer superior durability and protection for liquids and hazardous materials, making them suitable for more demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
