<p><ul><li>IC Card</li><li>Non-IC Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial single-phase electricity smart meter market is divided into two main types: IC Card and Non-IC Card. IC Card meters utilize integrated circuit cards for energy consumption tracking and user authentication, offering enhanced security and data management features. In contrast, Non-IC Card meters use traditional methods for monitoring energy usage without the need for a card, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types cater to industrial needs, with varying levels of technology and user engagement.</p></p>
