<p><p>Integrated cooker hoods equipped with rotary and centrifugal fans serve distinct purposes in kitchen ventilation. Rotary fan models provide a quieter operation and are ideal for smaller spaces, efficiently circulating air while minimizing noise levels. In contrast, centrifugal fans are designed for higher airflow, making them suitable for larger kitchens or professional settings where intense cooking generates significant smoke and odors. Both types enhance indoor air quality, with built-in designs offering seamless integration into kitchen aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
