<p><strong>The Intravenous (IV) Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Parenteral Solution</li><li>Peripheral Parenteral Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous (IV) solution market comprises two main types: Total Parenteral Solutions (TPN) and Peripheral Parenteral Solutions (PPN). TPN provides complete nutritional support directly into the bloodstream, typically for patients unable to consume food orally or through enteral routes. It contains carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals. In contrast, PPN delivers nutrients through peripheral veins, used for short-term needs or supplemental nutrition, usually in patients who can tolerate some oral feeding. Both types are essential in patient care management.</p></p>
