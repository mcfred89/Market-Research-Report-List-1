<p><p>The ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) sputtering targets market includes two main types: Plane Targets and Rotating Targets. Plane Targets are flat, stationary plates used in sputtering processes to deposit thin films on substrates, offering precise and uniform coatings. Rotating Targets are cylindrical and rotate during sputtering, providing longer operational life and higher material utilization efficiency. Both types are essential in producing transparent conductive films for applications like displays, solar panels, and touchscreens, with the choice depending on specific production needs and cost considerations.</p></p>
