<p><p>Light Fidelity (Li-Fi) and Visible Light Communication encompass a range of market types including LEDs, photodetectors, and microcontrollers (MCUs). LEDs serve as the primary light source for data transmission through modulated light signals. Photodetectors act as receivers, converting light signals back into electrical data. MCUs facilitate the processing and management of data transmission, enabling efficient communication. Together, these components create a robust framework for high-speed wireless communication using visible light, providing alternatives to traditional radio frequency technologies.</p></p>
