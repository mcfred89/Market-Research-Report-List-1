<p><p>The Magneto-Rheological Damper market is primarily divided into two types: active dampers and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize an external energy source to control the damping force dynamically, providing real-time adaptation to varying conditions. In contrast, semi-active dampers adjust their properties by changing the viscosity of the magneto-rheological fluid in response to magnetic fields, offering improved performance and energy efficiency while relying on existing system dynamics for control. Both types enhance ride comfort and safety in various applications.</p></p>
