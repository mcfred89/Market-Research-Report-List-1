<p><p>The marble countertops market is divided into two main types: natural and artificial. Natural marble is sourced directly from quarries, offering unique veining and coloration that adds elegance to spaces. It is prized for its beauty but may require more maintenance due to its porous nature. In contrast, artificial marble, often made from a blend of resins and stone particles, provides a more durable and customizable alternative, frequently resembling natural marble while being less susceptible to stains and chips.</p></p>
