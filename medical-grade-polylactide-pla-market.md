<p><strong>The Medical-grade Polylactide (PLA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medical L-Polylactide</li><li>Medical Racemic Polylactide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade polylactide (PLA) market primarily consists of two types: Medical L-Polylactide and Medical Racemic Polylactide. Medical L-Polylactide is synthesized from L-lactic acid, offering superior biocompatibility and biodegradability, making it ideal for applications like tissue engineering and drug delivery. In contrast, Medical Racemic Polylactide, derived from a mix of L- and D-lactic acid, provides distinct mechanical properties and degradation rates, suitable for various surgical applications, including sutures and implants. Both types are essential for advancing medical technology and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
