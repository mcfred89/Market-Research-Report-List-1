<p><p>The milk powder analyzers market comprises several types, primarily including Ultrasonic and NIR (Near-Infrared) milk analyzers, alongside other specialized models. Ultrasonic milk analyzers utilize sound waves to assess the quality and composition of milk powders, offering real-time measurements. NIR milk analyzers employ light reflection to determine fat, protein, and lactose content. Other analyzers may include technologies like Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) and Raman spectroscopy. Each type provides unique advantages in accuracy, speed, and versatility for dairy industry applications.</p></p>
