<p><p>The multi-channel spectrometer market comprises three primary types based on wavelength ranges: UV (Ultraviolet), VL (Visible Light), and NIR (Near-Infrared). UV spectrometers analyze short wavelengths for applications like chemical analysis and quality control. VL spectrometers focus on the visible spectrum for diverse fields, including biological studies and colorimetry. NIR spectrometers penetrate deeper into materials, making them invaluable in agriculture and pharmaceuticals for analyzing moisture content and chemical composition, thereby serving various industrial and research needs.</p></p>
