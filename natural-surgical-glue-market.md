<p><p>The natural surgical glue market encompasses various types, primarily fibrin, collagen, and gelatin. Fibrin glue, derived from blood components, promotes quick wound healing and is commonly used in surgical procedures. Collagen glue, sourced from animal tissues, aids in tissue adhesion and regeneration, making it suitable for reconstructive surgeries. Gelatin glue, a thermoplastic adhesive, offers versatility in applications, including wound closure and tissue repair. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing surgical outcomes and promoting patient recovery.</p></p>
