<p><p>Nondestructive Testing (NDT) encompasses various methods to assess material integrity without causing damage. Eddy Current Inspection detects flaws in conductive materials using electromagnetic induction. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials via magnetic fields. Electromagnetic Testing uses electric and magnetic fields to detect anomalies. Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface-breaking defects through dye penetration. Radiography employs X-rays or gamma rays to visualize internal structures. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws, ensuring material reliability across industries.</p></p>
