<p><ul><li>Magneto Optical Current Transformer (MOCT)</li><li>Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT)</li><li>Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (EFOCT)</li><li>Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FEFOCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Current Transformers (OCT) utilize optical technology for measuring electrical currents, offering high accuracy and safety advantages. The Magneto Optical Current Transformer (MOCT) employs magneto-optical effects for current measurement. Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT) uses optical fibers to sense current, enhancing immunity to electromagnetic interference. The Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (EFOCT) integrates electronic components with fiber optics for improved performance. Lastly, the Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FEFOCT) offers enhanced adaptability for installation in tight or unique locations, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
