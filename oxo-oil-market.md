<p><p>Oxo oil, also known as oxo-alcohols, is a group of chemicals derived from the hydroformylation or oxo process, which involves the reaction of olefins with synthesis gas (carbon monoxide and hydrogen). These oils are primarily used as raw materials in the production of plasticizers, solvents, adhesives, and coatings. The versatility of oxo oil makes it a critical component in industries such as automotive, construction, and packaging.</p><p>The Oxo Oil Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period.
