<p><p>The partial oxidation catalyst market is divided into activated and non-activated catalyst types. Activated catalysts enhance reaction rates and improve efficiency through surface activation, often utilizing metals or metal oxides. They are crucial for processes like methane conversion. Non-activated catalysts lack this enhancement, usually relying on thermal energy for reactions. While they may be less efficient, they are often simpler and more cost-effective for certain applications. Both types play vital roles in optimizing chemical processes under varying conditions.</p></p>
