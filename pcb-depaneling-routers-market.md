<p><p>The PCB depaneling routers market can be categorized into two main types: in-line and off-line depaneling routers. In-line depaneling routers are integrated into the automated production line, enabling seamless processing of PCBs directly after the soldering phase, which enhances operational efficiency and reduces handling time. Off-line depaneling routers, on the other hand, operate independently of the production line, allowing for batch processing of PCBs, offering flexibility and convenience for smaller production runs or specialized tasks.</p></p>
