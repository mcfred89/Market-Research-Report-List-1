<p><p>The plastic pipette tip market is segmented into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are commonly used for general applications in laboratories, offering accuracy and precision without additional filtration. In contrast, filtered pipette tips incorporate a barrier that prevents contamination from aerosols and liquids, making them ideal for sensitive applications involving volatile or hazardous substances. This differentiation helps laboratories choose the appropriate tip based on their specific sample handling and contamination prevention needs.</p></p>
