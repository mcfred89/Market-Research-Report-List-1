<p><p>The Portable Surgical Suction Pumps market is segmented into two main types: plug-in surgical suction pumps and battery-operated surgical suction pumps. Plug-in pumps are powered by a direct electrical source, providing continuous suction for prolonged procedures, making them ideal for use in hospitals and surgical centers. In contrast, battery surgical suction pumps offer mobility and are often used in field settings or emergency situations, enabling healthcare professionals to perform suctioning without being tethered to an electrical outlet.</p></p>
