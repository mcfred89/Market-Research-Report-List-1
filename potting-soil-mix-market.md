<p><p>The potting soil mix market comprises several types, including soilless mixes, composts, gravel, inert media, and others. Soilless mixes are specifically designed for optimal drainage and aeration, often used in container gardening. Composts provide nutrient-rich organic matter for enhanced plant growth. Gravel is utilized for drainage and as a base layer in potting. Inert media, such as perlite or vermiculite, offer lightweight alternatives with good aeration. Other mixes may include specialized blends tailored for specific plant needs or conditions.</p></p>
