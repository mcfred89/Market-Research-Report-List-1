<p><ul><li>Acid Phytase</li><li>Alkaline Phytase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Powder Phytases market is primarily segmented into two types: Acid Phytase and Alkaline Phytase. Acid Phytases are effective in low pH environments, typically found in the stomachs of monogastric animals, enhancing phosphorus absorption from feed. In contrast, Alkaline Phytases function optimally at higher pH levels, making them suitable for the gastrointestinal tracts of ruminants and poultry. Both types serve to improve nutrient availability in animal feed, reduce environmental phosphorus waste, and support sustainable livestock farming practices.</p></p>
