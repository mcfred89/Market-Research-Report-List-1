<p><p>The Pyroelectric Effect Detector market comprises several types. The Thermoelectric Type utilizes temperature differences to generate an electrical signal. The Thermistor Type is based on resistance changes with temperature variations, providing high sensitivity. Pneumatic detectors operate by measuring pressure changes due to temperature fluctuations, often used in gas detection. Lastly, the Pyroelectric Type directly converts thermal energy into an electric charge when exposed to infrared radiation, making it ideal for motion detection in security systems and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
