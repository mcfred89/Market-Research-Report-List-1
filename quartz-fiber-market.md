<p><p>The Quartz Fiber market includes various types such as Yarns, Rovings, Felts, Sewing Thread, and Others. Yarns are continuous strands of quartz fibers used in weaving and reinforcement. Rovings are bundles of untwisted fibers, ideal for composite materials. Felts are non-woven mats of quartz fibers, offering thermal insulation and filtration. Sewing Thread consists of fine quartz fibers for high-temperature stitching. Other products include custom forms like tapes or braids, catering to specialized industrial applications requiring heat resistance and durability.</p></p>
