<p><p>The refractory magnesia market comprises several types, including Fused Magnesia, Dead Burned Magnesia (DBM), and others. Fused Magnesia is produced by melting magnesium oxide in electric furnaces and is known for its high purity and density, ideal for high-temperature applications. Dead Burned Magnesia is created by calcining magnesium carbonate and is used primarily in refractory applications due to its excellent stability and low reactivity. Other types may include lighter or specialized formulations catering to varying industrial needs.</p></p>
