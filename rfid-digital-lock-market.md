<p><p>The RFID digital lock market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless locks. Contact RFID locks require physical contact between the key fob or card and the reader, ensuring secure access but often limiting convenience. In contrast, contactless RFID locks allow users to unlock doors without physical contact, enhancing user experience and speed of access. Both types provide increased security and efficiency in various applications, from residential to commercial environments, catering to different user preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
