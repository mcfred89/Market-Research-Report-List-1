<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID Reader</li><li>HF RFID Reader</li><li>UHF RFID Reader</li><li>MW RFID Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID equipment market is categorized into four main types based on frequency: </p><p>1. **LF RFID Reader** (Low Frequency, 125-134 kHz) is used for access control and animal tracking due to its short read range.  </p><p>2. **HF RFID Reader** (High Frequency, 13.56 MHz) is commonly used in smart cards and NFC applications.  </p><p>3. **UHF RFID Reader** (Ultra High Frequency, 860-960 MHz) enables longer read distances and faster data transfer, ideal for supply chain and inventory management.  </p><p>4. **MW RFID Reader** (Microwave Frequency) operates at frequencies beyond 1 GHz, offering high-speed data transfer for specialized applications.</p></p>
