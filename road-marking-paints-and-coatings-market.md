Thermoplastic marking paint is durable and heat-applied, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. Water-based marking paint offers low VOC emissions and ease of application, suitable for environmentally conscious projects. Solvent-based marking paint provides strong adhesion and quick drying but contains higher VOC levels. Two-component road marking paint features a reactive system for enhanced durability and resistance, making it suitable for specialized applications and extreme conditions.</p></p>
