<p><p>Sales platform software is typically categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on the vendor’s servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. On-premises software, conversely, is installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring a larger initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve to enhance sales processes, improve customer relationships, and drive revenue growth.</p></p>
