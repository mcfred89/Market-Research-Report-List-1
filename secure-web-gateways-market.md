<p><p>Secure Web Gateways (SWGs) can be categorized into two primary market types: On-Premises and Cloud. </p><p>On-Premises SWGs are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing direct control over security policies and network traffic management. This type suits organizations with strict compliance requirements or existing infrastructure investments. </p><p>Cloud SWGs, on the other hand, operate through a hosted service, offering scalability and ease of deployment. They are ideal for businesses seeking flexibility, rapid updates, and lower maintenance burdens. Both options enhance web security, ensuring safe internet access.</p></p>
