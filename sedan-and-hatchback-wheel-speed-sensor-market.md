<p><p>The Sedan and Hatchback Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises two primary types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed measurements. They are often favored for their accuracy and reliability in various driving conditions. Magnetic Electric Type sensors, on the other hand, operate based on changes in magnetic fields generated by the wheel's motion, converting this information into electrical signals. Both types are essential for advanced vehicle safety and traction control systems.</p></p>
