<p><p>A single scull is a narrow, lightweight rowing boat designed for one person, who uses two oars (one in each hand) to propel the boat forward. The single scull is popular among both amateur and professional rowers due to its versatility and the physical and mental challenges it presents.</p><p>The Single Scull Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing participation in rowing as a sport and fitness activity.
