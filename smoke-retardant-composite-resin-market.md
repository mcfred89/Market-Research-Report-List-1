<p><p>The smoke retardant composite resin market comprises three primary types: phenolic, epoxy, and polyester. Phenolic resins offer excellent fire resistance and low smoke generation, making them ideal for aerospace and transportation applications. Epoxy resins provide superior mechanical properties and chemical resistance, widely used in electrical and automotive sectors. Polyester resins, though less fire-resistant than the other two, are cost-effective and commonly used in construction and marine applications. Each type serves unique needs based on performance, application, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
