<p><p>The synthetic surgical glove market is categorized into three main types: polyisoprene, neoprene, and nitrile. Polyisoprene gloves mimic natural latex in comfort and elasticity, making them suitable for sensitive tasks. Neoprene gloves offer enhanced chemical resistance and are ideal for environments where exposure to harmful substances occurs. Nitrile gloves are known for their durability and puncture resistance, making them a popular choice in surgical procedures where safety is paramount. Each type caters to specific needs within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
