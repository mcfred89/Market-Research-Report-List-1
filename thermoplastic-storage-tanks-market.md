<p><p>The thermoplastic storage tanks market is primarily categorized into single wall and double wall tanks. Single wall tanks consist of a single layer of thermoplastic material, offering basic containment for liquids and chemicals, suitable for less hazardous applications. In contrast, double wall tanks feature an inner and outer layer, providing enhanced protection against leaks and environmental contamination, making them ideal for hazardous substances. The choice between these types depends on the specific safety requirements and regulatory standards of the application.</p></p>
