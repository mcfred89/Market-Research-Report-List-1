<p><strong>The Turboexpander Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Turboexpander</li><li>Axial Turboexpander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turboexpander compressor market is categorized into two main types: radial turboexpanders and axial turboexpanders. Radial turboexpanders utilize a centrifugal design, ideal for high-pressure applications with efficient performance in smaller footprints. They are commonly used in cryogenics and refrigeration. In contrast, axial turboexpanders feature a more elongated design and excel in high-volume gas flows, making them suitable for power generation and large-scale industrial processes. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency and optimizing fluid dynamics in various sectors.</p></p>
