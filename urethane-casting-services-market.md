<p><p>Urethane casting services encompass two primary market types: flexible and rigid urethane materials. Flexible urethane materials are designed for applications requiring elasticity and resilience, making them ideal for items like gaskets, seals, and cushioning components. In contrast, rigid urethane materials offer high strength and durability, suitable for structural components, housings, and molds. Both types cater to diverse industries, providing customizable solutions that meet specific performance and aesthetic requirements, while ensuring efficient production and cost-effectiveness in various applications.</p></p>
