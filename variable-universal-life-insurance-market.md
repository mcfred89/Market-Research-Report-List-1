<p><p>Variable Universal Life Insurance (VUL) comprises two primary market types: Flexible Premium and Fixed Premium. Flexible Premium VUL allows policyholders to adjust their premium payments and invest in various sub-accounts for potential cash value growth. This flexibility appeals to those seeking customization and investment opportunities. In contrast, Fixed Premium VUL requires a consistent premium payment, providing more predictable costs and often appealing to those who prefer stability over fluctuating investment returns. Both types offer life coverage with investment potential.</p></p>
