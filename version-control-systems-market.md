<p><p>Version control systems (VCS) are categorized into two main markets: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise VCS solutions are installed and managed within an organization's infrastructure, offering enhanced control over data security and compliance. They are ideal for businesses with specific regulatory requirements. Conversely, cloud-based VCS solutions are hosted remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any location. They typically promote collaboration among distributed teams and reduce the burden on internal IT management, making them suitable for agile environments.</p></p>
