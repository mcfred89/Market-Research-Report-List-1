<p><strong>The Wireless VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless VOC detector market is primarily segmented into two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID technology utilizes ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, providing rapid and highly sensitive detection, ideal for hazardous environments. In contrast, MOS sensors operate through changes in electrical resistance when exposed to VOCs, offering a more cost-effective solution for continuous monitoring. Both technologies are crucial in various applications, including industrial safety, environmental monitoring, and indoor air quality management.</p></p>
